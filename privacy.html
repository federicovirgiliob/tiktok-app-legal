Privacy Policy

Last updated: 05/05/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how test12345 ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our services, specifically through our integration with the TikTok Developer Platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our App via TikTok Login, we may access the following information based on the permissions (scopes) you grant:

  • Profile Information: Basic public profile details.
  • Statistics (user.info.stats): Data regarding your followers count, following count, likes, and video count.
  • Video List (video.list): Information about the public videos you have posted on TikTok.
  • Content Posting: If authorized, the App may upload video content to your TikTok account as a draft or direct post.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected strictly for the following purposes:

  • AI Analysis: Our AI agent processes your video performance and profile statistics to provide insights and content recommendations.
  • Automated Posting: To facilitate the scheduling and publishing of video content as requested by you.
  • Service Improvement: To enhance the functionality and user experience of our App.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information when necessary to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the services requested. You can revoke our App's access to your TikTok account at any time.

5. How to Revoke Access

You can manage or revoke our App's access to your data directly within the TikTok app:

  1. Open TikTok and go to your Profile.
  2. Tap the Menu icon (three lines) and go to Settings and Privacy.
  3. Select Security and login (or Security).
  4. Tap Manage app permissions and find test12345 to revoke access.
